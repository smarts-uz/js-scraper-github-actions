What is Coral?

Coral is the conversational AI assistant from Cohere, powered by their Command model. This tool enables users to engage in complex conversations on any subject, ask questions, and get answers in a natural and engaging way. With Coral, you can create lists, summarize documents, build itineraries, find strategies, and receive step-by-step instructions, all through conversation. It’s like having a knowledgeable and intelligent friend by your side to answer questions and provide insights as you explore a new topic. With Coral, you can get the help you need faster and more efficiently, without having to search through endless amounts of data. Experience the power of conversational AI and unlock the true potential of your conversations with Coral.

Use Cases And Features

1. Create interactive conversations on any topic with Coral.
2. Ask questions and get natural and engaging responses.
3. Use Coral to summarize documents, build itineraries, and receive step-by-step instructions.
